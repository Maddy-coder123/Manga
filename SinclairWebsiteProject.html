What is Manga and why is it so popular?

Manga is best known as a Japanese form of art that are turned into books, manga in Japanese means comic. Manga has been around ever since the 19th century, with the first manga titaled The Water Sprinkaler which was created in 1902. Manga is very popular in Japan because of its beautiful story lines, character designs, and pretty art.
